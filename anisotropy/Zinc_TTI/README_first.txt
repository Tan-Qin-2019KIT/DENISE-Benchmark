If you use the anisotropic zinc model for your own research please cite the following reference in your 
publications:

Carcione, J. M., Kosloff, D. and R. Kosloff, 1988 
Wave-propagation simulation in an elastic anisotropic (transversely 
isotropic) solid. Quarterly Journal of Mechanics and Applied Mathematics,
41, 319–345.

Komatitsch, D., Barnes, C. and J. Tromp, 2000 
Simulation of anisotropic wave propagation based upon a spectral element method. 
Geophysics, 65, 1251–1260.

Operto, S., Virieux, J., Ribodetti, A. and J. E. Anderson, 2009 
Finite-difference frequency-domain modeling of visco-acoustic 
wave propagation in two-dimensional TTI media. 
Geophysics, 74 (5), T75-T95.
